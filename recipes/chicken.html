Chicken Fajitsa Recipe


Ingredients s:-

  • 2 large chicken breasts, finely sliced
  • 1 red onion, finely sliced
  • 1 red pepper, sliced
  • 1 red pepper, sliced

Method:-

  1. step

    2. Heat oven to 200C/180C fan/gas 6 and

    wrap 6 medium tortillas in foil.

  2. step

    3. Mix 1 heaped tbsp smoked paprika, 1

    tbsp ground coriander, a pinch of ground

    cumin, 2 crushed garlic cloves, 4 tbsp

    olive oil, the juice of 1 lime and 4-5 drops

    olive oil, the juice of 1 lime and 4-5 drops

    Tabasco together in a bowl with a big

    pinch each of salt and pepper.

  3. step

    4. Stir 2 finely sliced chicken breasts, 1

    finely sliced red onion, 1 sliced red

    pepper and 1 finely sliced red chilli, if

    using, into the marinade.

  4. step

    5. Heat a griddle pan until smoking hot

    and add the chicken and marinade to the

    pan.

  5. step

    6. Keep everything moving over a high heat

    for about 5 mins using tongs until you

    get a nice charred effect. If your griddle

    pan is small you may need to do this in

    two batches.

  6. step

    7. To check the chicken is cooked, find the

    thickest part and tear in half if any part

    is still raw cook until done.

  7. step

    8. Put the tortillas in the oven to heat up

    and serve with the cooked chicken, a bag

    of mixed salad and one 230g tub of fresh

    salsa.
